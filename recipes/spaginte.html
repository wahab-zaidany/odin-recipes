LIBYAN PASTA

Pasta Recipe

NOTE:

it will be more delicious if you make it on a wood fire

Description

So our dish today is Libyan pasta, maybe you think it is like any pasta but it is quite different.

the pasta in Libya is very popular we make it anytime and anywhere

Here we will learn how to make pasta with Libyan taste.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (any type you like but we prefer spaghetti)
  • Tomato
  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • meat

Steps

    bring big bowl put some oil in it cut the Tomato and the Onion put the Spices put the meat
  1. give it 5 minutes
  2. put water until the half of the bowl
  4. and then wite until the meat is done
  5. then take off the meat
  6. and but the pasta
  7. give it 5 minutes to cook
  8. your pasta is ready
