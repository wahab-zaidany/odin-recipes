Spagite Recipe

Description

So our dish today is spagite or like italyan called (pasta).

It is an italyan food and easy to make but many people do not know how to make it until now.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Oil
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • meat

Steps

    bring big bowl but some oil in it cut the Tomato and the Onion but the Spices but the meat
  1. give it 5 minutes
  2. but some water
  3. and then wite until the meat is done
  4. then take off the meat
  5. and but the pasta
  6. give it 5 minutes to cook
  7. your pasta is ready
